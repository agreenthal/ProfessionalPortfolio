Last Minute Date Generator

Is it the weekend? Did you forget to make plans for that special someone? Use the Last Minute Date Generator to solve your problem!

About the App!

For our first project, we were tasked with building an application that incorporated everything we learned in the first 4 weeks of the bootcamp. With this taking place around the time of Valentines Day, we decided to make an app that might be useful to people in the real world. Simply enter your location and what kind of entertainment & cuisine you would like, and the Last Minute Date Generator will provide you with a highly rated restaurant and an event happening in your area.
